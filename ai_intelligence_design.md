# Kulima Africa: AI Intelligence Layer Design

## 1. AI Architecture: The "Decision Infrastructure"
AI in Kulima Africa is not a peripheral feature; it is the central nervous system that orchestrates agriculture, housing, and finance.

### Layers:
- **Perception Layer (Data Ingestion)**:
  - **IoT Streams**: Soil moisture, nitrogen levels, satellite imagery (NDVI).
  - **Interaction Streams**: WhatsApp logs (LUMOZA), App navigation, Search queries.
  - **Financial Streams**: Transaction history, voucher usage, repayment rates.
- **Cognition Layer (Gemini-Powered)**:
  - **Reasoning**: Gemini 3.1 Pro for complex agricultural planning and mortgage eligibility.
  - **Real-time Processing**: Gemini 3 Flash for fraud detection and instant recommendations.
- **Action Layer (Autonomous Agents)**:
  - **Auto-Disbursement**: Triggering vouchers based on verified data.
  - **Proactive Alerts**: Notifying users of market shifts or weather risks.

---

## 2. Core Intelligence Engines

### A. KulimaVerse: Impact Trust Scoring (ITS)
**Logic**: Moves beyond traditional credit scores (based on debt) to "Impact Scores" (based on productivity).
- **Inputs**: Verified harvest volume, soil health improvement, community peer-reviews, financial consistency.
- **Decision Logic**: `Score = (0.4 * Productivity) + (0.3 * Financial_Reliability) + (0.2 * Community_Verification) + (0.1 * Environmental_Impact)`.
- **Outcome**: Unlocks lower interest rates in LUNDAI and higher credit limits in the Wallet.

### B. LUMOZA: Agronomy & Yield Prediction
**Logic**: Real-time advisor for maximizing output.
- **Inputs**: Local weather forecasts, historical yield data, real-time sensor data.
- **Decision Logic**: If `Soil_Moisture < 30%` AND `Forecast == No_Rain`, trigger `Irrigation_Alert` + `Power_Voucher_Check`.

### C. LUNDAI: Mortgage Readiness Engine
**Logic**: Predicts long-term financial stability for housing.
- **Inputs**: 3-year projected agricultural yield, current trust score, regional property trends.
- **Decision Logic**: Analyzes the "Harvest-to-Home" ratio. If projected income covers 3x mortgage payments, auto-approve for "Eco-Home" financing.

---

## 3. Fraud Detection & Security
- **Identity Verification**: Multi-modal biometric matching (Face + Voice) via Gemini Vision/Audio.
- **Anomaly Detection**: Real-time monitoring of transaction velocity and location.
- **Social Proofing**: Cross-referencing agricultural claims with satellite imagery to prevent "Ghost Harvest" fraud.

---

## 4. Personalization & Recommendation
- **Cross-Platform Engine**: If a user searches for "Maize Seeds" in LUMOZA, LUNDAI suggests "Warehouse Storage" options, and the Wallet offers a "Seed Loan."
- **Behavioral Modeling**: Learns the user's "Life Rhythm" (e.g., when they typically buy supplies) to surface the right vouchers at the right time.

## 5. Example Scenarios

### Scenario 1: The Proactive Harvest
1. **AI Agent** detects a 15% rise in Maize prices in a neighboring region via market data.
2. **AI Agent** checks the user's harvest readiness via satellite imagery.
3. **Action**: Sends a notification: *"Market prices are peaking. I've reserved a transport slot for your harvest. Confirm to lock in the +15% profit."*

### Scenario 2: The Trust-Based Mortgage
1. User completes their 5th successful verified harvest cycle.
2. **KulimaVerse** updates the Trust Score to 850+.
3. **LUNDAI Agent** automatically generates a personalized mortgage offer for a modern eco-home.
4. **Action**: Dashboard displays: *"Your harvest success has unlocked a 4.5% interest rate. View your dream home."*
