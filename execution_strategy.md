# Kulima Africa: Execution & Scaling Strategy (Malawi First)

## 1. 🔥 Launch Strategy: The "Trojan Horse" Sequence

### Phase 1: LUMOZA (Month 1)
- **Why**: WhatsApp has 95% penetration in Malawi. No app download required. Immediate utility (weather, pest control).
- **Goal**: Capture the "Daily Active User" (DAU) through utility.
- **Metric**: Number of successful "Advice-to-Action" loops.

### Phase 2: KulimaVerse (Month 2-3)
- **Why**: Once farmers use LUMOZA, we have data. We turn that data into "Impact Trust Scores" (ITS).
- **Goal**: Start verifying harvests and building the "Social Collateral" needed for finance.
- **Metric**: Number of "Verified Farmers".

### Phase 3: LUNDAI (Month 6+)
- **Why**: Housing is the ultimate aspiration. It requires high trust and high capital.
- **Goal**: Convert top-tier farmers into homeowners.
- **Metric**: First 10 "Harvest-to-Home" mortgages.

---

## 2. 🧭 Integration Strategy: The Unified OS

- **Step 1 (Immediate)**: Shared Identity (Kulima ID). A user on LUMOZA (WhatsApp) is the same user in the Kulima App.
- **Step 2 (Month 2)**: The Wallet. Connect LUMOZA's advice to the Wallet's vouchers. (e.g., "AI says you need Fertilizer -> Here is a 10% discount voucher in your Wallet").
- **Step 3 (Month 4)**: KulimaVerse API. LUNDAI starts reading ITS scores to pre-approve mortgages.

---

## 3. 👥 User Acquisition Plan

- **First 100 Users**: "The Ground Game". Direct partnerships with 2 farmer cooperatives in Lilongwe. Manual onboarding.
- **First 1,000 Users**: "The Viral Loop". Referral bonuses in the Wallet. "Refer a neighbor, get 500 MWK in airtime/vouchers".
- **First 10,000 Users**: "The Input Hook". Partner with seed/fertilizer companies. They distribute Kulima ID cards with every purchase.

---

## 4. ⚙️ Operational Strategy: "Do Things That Don't Scale"

- **Manual (Day 1)**: KYC document review, manual verification of harvest photos, customer support via WhatsApp.
- **Do NOT Automate Yet**: The "Trust Verification" in KulimaVerse. Use human "Village Ambassadors" to verify big claims initially.

---

## 5. 💰 Revenue Strategy (Early Cashflow)

- **B2B Commissions**: Charge input suppliers (seeds/fertilizer) a 3-5% lead-gen fee for every voucher redeemed.
- **Transaction Fees**: Small 1% fee on P2P transfers within the Wallet.
- **Delay**: Interest on mortgages and premium AI features. Focus on volume first.

---

## 6. 🚫 What NOT To Do

- **Don't build a heavy app**: Keep the initial app under 15MB.
- **Don't ignore the "Last Mile"**: If the AI says "buy fertilizer" but there's no fertilizer in the village, you lose trust.
- **Don't over-automate trust**: One bad actor can ruin the protocol. Keep human checks in the loop for high-value claims.

---

## 7. 📆 30-60-90 Day Plan

- **Day 30**: LUMOZA Live in Lilongwe. 500 active farmers.
- **Day 60**: Kulima ID + Wallet Launch. First 100 "Verified" profiles in KulimaVerse.
- **Day 90**: First "Impact Loan" disbursed via KulimaVerse. LUNDAI Beta opens for top 50 farmers.
