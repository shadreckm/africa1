# Kulima Africa: Malawi Launch Execution Plan (The Ground Game)

## 1. Target User Profile: "The Progressive Smallholder"
*   **Name**: "Bambo/Mayi Phiri"
*   **Location**: Lilongwe Rural (Mitundu/Msundwe) or Dedza.
*   **Crops**: Maize (staple), Soya or Groundnuts (cash crops).
*   **Phone**: Itel A56 or Tecno Pop series (Android 9/10).
*   **Behavior**: Member of a VSLA (Village Savings and Loan Association). Uses WhatsApp for family and church groups.

---

## 2. Acquisition Strategy: The Ground Level
### Where to find them:
1.  **Msundwe Market (Wednesday/Saturday)**: High traffic of farmers selling produce.
2.  **Local VSLAs**: Trusted peer groups where financial decisions happen.
3.  **Seed/Fertilizer Retailers**: Points of high intent.

### The Field Script (Chichewa/English Hybrid):
> "Moni Bambo! Kodi mukufuna kudziwa momwe mungakulitsire zokolola zanu chaka chino? (Hello! Do you want to know how to grow more harvest this year?)
> 
> LUMOZA is your new AI farming assistant on WhatsApp. It tells you the best time to plant, how to fix sick crops, and helps you save for a better house. It's free to start. Just send 'Moni' to this number."

---

## 3. Onboarding Flow: Zero Friction
1.  **Physical Flyer**: A simple A5 paper with a large WhatsApp QR code and the number +265...
2.  **The "Moni" Hook**: User sends "Moni".
3.  **Auto-Reply**: "Welcome to LUMOZA! What are you growing today? 1. Maize 2. Soya 3. Tobacco".
4.  **The First Value**: User selects "Maize". LUMOZA immediately sends the 3-day weather forecast for their specific village.

---

## 4. Retention Strategy: The Daily Habit
*   **Daily Weather & Pest Alerts**: Sent every morning at 6:00 AM.
*   **Market Price Updates**: Every Friday, showing prices in Lilongwe vs. Blantyre.
*   **The "Impact Point" (VU)**: Every time they log an activity (e.g., "I applied fertilizer today"), they earn 5 VU points.

---

## 5. Incentive System: Airtime & Vouchers
*   **Referral**: "Invite 3 friends to LUMOZA and get K500 Airtime (TNM/Airtel) instantly."
*   **Voucher**: "Log your harvest for 4 weeks and get a 10% discount voucher at [Local Agro-Dealer]."

---

## 6. Manual Operations: "Wizard of Oz" Scaling
*   **The "AI" is You**: For the first 100 users, a human (Field Operator) reviews every crop photo sent to LUMOZA to ensure the diagnosis is 100% correct before the AI replies.
*   **Manual Airtime**: Use a dedicated phone to manually send airtime rewards to users who hit referral targets.
*   **Weekly Village Visits**: Visit the first 100 users in person to see how they use the tool.

---

## 7. Success Metrics (The First 100)
*   **Primary**: % of users who send at least 3 messages per week.
*   **Secondary**: Number of crop photos uploaded.
*   **Ignore**: Total number of "Moni" messages (vanity metric).

---

## 8. What NOT to Do
*   **DO NOT** build a mobile app. Data is too expensive; WhatsApp is already paid for in "bundles".
*   **DO NOT** use complex English. Use simple Chichewa or Tumbuka.
*   **DO NOT** ignore the Village Headman. Always ask for permission before gathering farmers.

---

## 9. Execution Checklist (Daily)
*   [ ] **06:00**: Send automated weather/tip of the day.
*   [ ] **09:00 - 14:00**: Field visits (Markets/Cooperatives). Target: 10 new signups.
*   [ ] **16:00**: Review all user queries from the day.
*   [ ] **18:00**: Manually process airtime rewards for referrals.

---

## 10. Weekly Goals
*   **Week 1**: 20 users. Focus on 1 VSLA group.
*   **Week 2**: 50 users. Start the referral system.
*   **Week 3**: 80 users. Collect first 10 crop "success stories".
*   **Week 4**: 100 users. Analyze retention data.
